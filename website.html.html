About Us

Established in 2024, Sri Gnanambika Girls Junior College is committed to empowering young girls through quality education and competitive exam excellence. Located in Bose Nagar, Kothapeta, Rayachoty, our mission is to create confident, knowledgeable, and successful future professionals.
